—ABOUT ME—

A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME...

Hi there! My name is Amie Villiger. I am a graphic designer, a digital illustrator, and oil painter. I earned my BFA and BA in Spring of 2013 from Indiana University Southeast. I live in the beautiful Highlands of Louisville, Kentucky with my fiancee and our dogs. When I'm not making stuff, I enjoy hiking, camping, sailing, and playing video and board games.


DESIGN

I love to design. I love to illustrate. I love to create. I've been drawing since I was old enough to hold a pencil. I pride myself in being easy to work with and always hardworking. I enjoy tight deadlines, multitasking, and making an impact with my work. I've done design work for many non-profit organizations and local businesses, everything ranging from web design, CD packaging, and academic journals, to logos, t-shirts, and a digital interactive historical timeline.
